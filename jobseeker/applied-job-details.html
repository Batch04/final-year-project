About the Role

We are seeking a motivated and creative Part-Time Marketing Assistant to join our dynamic team at TechStart Solutions. This is an excellent opportunity for someone looking to gain hands-on experience in digital marketing while maintaining flexibility for other commitments such as studies or personal projects.

As a Marketing Assistant, you will work closely with our marketing team to support various campaigns, content creation, and customer engagement initiatives. This role offers great learning opportunities and the potential for growth within our organization.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in creating and scheduling social media content across multiple platforms
  • Support email marketing campaigns and newsletter creation
  • Conduct market research and competitor analysis
  • Help maintain and update company website content
  • Assist with event planning and promotional activities
  • Create basic graphics and visual content using design tools
  • Monitor and report on marketing campaign performance

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or related field (or currently pursuing)
  • 1-2 years of experience in marketing or related field (internships count)
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and Google Workspace
  • Basic knowledge of social media platforms and digital marketing
  • Creative thinking and attention to detail
  • Ability to work independently and manage time effectively

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with design tools (Canva, Adobe Creative Suite)
  • Knowledge of SEO and content marketing principles
  • Familiarity with marketing automation tools
  • Previous experience in a startup environment
